About

JobGenie is a platform built to bring job seekers and recruiters together in a smarter, more personalized way.

For candidates, JobGenie offers customizable profiles where skills and experiences can shine, making it easier to stand out to potential employers. Job listings can be searched and filtered to match personal preferences, helping candidates quickly discover the right opportunities.

For recruiters, JobGenie provides simple tools to post openings with detailed requirements and review applications efficiently. Insights into candidate distribution make it easier to identify top talent and make data-driven hiring decisions.
