About

JobGenie is a platform designed to connect job seekers with recruiters. Candidates have the ability to personalize their profiles and showcase their skills to attract employers. Employers can utilize the site to publish job listings with important specifications and review applications to find the person that best fits their needs.

JobGenie allows for a user-friendly interface to allow for easy connections between the recruiters and candidates. Candidates are able to search for jobs that fit their preferences with ease whilst recruiters are able to visualize candidate distrubtion.
